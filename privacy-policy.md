This app, Tic Tac Toe AI, is provided as-is and is intended for entertainment purposes.

## Data Collection
The app does not require user registration and does not collect personal data directly.

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads.
AdMob may collect anonymous usage data such as:
- Device information

These data are used only to provide relevant ads.
